Sentinels

Every time new coordinates are calculated by adding an offset, the resulting coordinates, in principle, have to be checked to see if they are within the grid.

In the reconstruction, sentinels are used, which in this case means putting a wall round the outside of the level grid. Although there is still an out-of-bounds test, it is in one place and it results in an error being reported.

The surrounding wall need not necessarily appear either in the level file or on screen, but it turned out to be convenient for it to be included in both. A little translation program was used to convert the level files to add the border, in addition to other minor changes.
