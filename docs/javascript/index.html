design notes

Wanderer was written by Steven Shipway in the 1980's. See The original credits.
This reconstruction was written by Ian Holyer in October 2011. The controls are:

arrow keys   to move (or h,j,k,l or w,a,s,d)      space bar to stand still

To play the game, collect all the stars and then go through the exit. The entities you will meet are:

This is you when you lose.
This is you. Exit - must collect all the stars first.
Solid wall. Solid rock.
Passable earth (score 1). Star. Collect them all before exiting (score 10).
Arrow, running left. Deflector. Boulders etc. slide past.
Arrow, running right. Deflector.
Landmine. Instant annihilation Teleport (score 20).
Boulder (falls down, other boulders and arrows slide round it). Monster (eats you up whole. Yum Yum yum..) (kill with a rock or arrow, score 100).
Balloon - rises, and is popped by arrows. It does not kill you. Baby monster (kills you, but doesn't chase you). Capture it in a cage (score 20) to turn it into a star.
Time capsule, giving 250 extra moves (score 5). Cage - traps baby monsters.
Space: this makes the icon available to JavaScript.
